The CHORDS Server

Create your personal CHORDS server in the cloud where you can observe and manage your data with ease. Manage your server with a browser and use Grafana to customize, view, and sort your data.

Submit and Monitor Data

Simple HTTP queries send your real-time data to your CHORDS server.Simple HTTP requests send your real-time data to your CHORDS server. You can view your data and instrument performance in your browser and easily detect instrument problems or system performance issues.

Stream Data Out

Download your data from the server using web forms or HTTP requests. Pull data from your CHORDS server into your personal programs with a few easy lines of code. Build monitoring applications to automatically track your measurements. Access your data from other standards-based cloud services. CHORDS will provide automatic registration functions for connecting to visualization, mapping, data federation, and other services.


The CHORDS project is inspired and funded by the National Science Foundation EarthCube initiative.
